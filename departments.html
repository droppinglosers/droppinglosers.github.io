    Enver Creek is home to many departments. Below is a list of programs that you can join! Have fun!

Math

The Math Department is a pride of Enver Creek. Students participate in multiple levels of mathematics course and often compete in national contests.

English

Our English department prides itself on a self-published book each year, featuring student work. Students are also invited to take our new Senior program featuring many different types of courses.

Physical and Health Education

Physical and Health Education is an important part of life-long phyisal acitivity. Students who are physically educated live healthier and happier lives; the PHE department is the best place to start.
